\begin{equation*}
\begin{split}
    t & = \frac{\overline{x} - \mu}{s / \sqrt{n}}\\
    & = \frac{101-98}{15/\sqrt{14}}\\
    & = .05
\end{split}
\end{equation*}

sig level = .01\\
From t-table $\rightarrow 3.012 > |.05|$ \\
So pass \\ \\ \\
